abstract: >
  This study proposes a patient-specific coronary flow prediction model using
  Physics-Informed Neural Operators (PINO) to overcome limitations of traditional
  CFD and PINNs. The model integrates a PointNet encoder with DeepONet architecture
  to learn the mapping from vessel geometry to flow fields governed by Navier-Stokes
  equations. Trained on 16 patient-specific coronary geometries from fused OCT-CT
  images, the PINO model accurately reconstructs 3D pressure and velocity fields
  with high consistency to CFD ground truth.
